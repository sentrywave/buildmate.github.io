Our Brick Types

Compressed Bricks

High-density bricks made with hydraulic pressure for superior strength and durability.

Best for: Modern construction, load-bearing walls

Common Bricks

Traditional clay bricks fired in kilns for general construction purposes.

Best for: Internal walls, partitions, general masonry

Load Bearing Bricks

Specially designed to support heavy structural loads in buildings.

Best for: Multi-story buildings, foundations

Face Bricks

High-quality bricks with attractive appearance for visible surfaces.

Best for: Exterior walls, feature walls, facades

Botswana Bricks

Premium quality bricks imported from Botswana known for their durability.

Best for: High-end construction, prestige projects

Brick Selection Guide

Choosing the right bricks for your project:

  • Structural Needs: Load bearing bricks for supporting structures
  • Aesthetics: Face bricks for visible, attractive surfaces
  • Budget: Common bricks for cost-effective construction
  • Durability: Compressed or Botswana bricks for long-lasting results
  • Climate: Consider weather resistance for exterior use

Our experts can help you select the perfect bricks for your specific needs.
